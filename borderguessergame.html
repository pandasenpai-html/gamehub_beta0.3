GAME RULES

"Country Shape Guessr" is an exciting and educational game that tests your knowledge of world geography. In each round, you'll be shown the outline shape of a country, and your goal is to correctly identify which country it represents. As you advance, you'll encounter shapes of countries from around the world and earn points for each correct guess. You can submit your answer, skip if you're unsure, or request a hint (with a small score penalty).

Game Rules

Objective:

Identify the country represented by its outline shape to earn points. Strive for the highest score by the end of the game.

Guessing the Country:

  • Type your guess into the input box and press the Submit Guess button.
  • A correct guess earns you a point and advances you to the next country shape.
  • If your guess is incorrect, you can try again without losing points.

Skipping:

  • If you're unsure, click the Skip button.
  • Skipping reveals the correct answer but doesn't award points. You'll proceed to the next country shape afterward.

Hints:

  • Click the Hint button for a helpful clue about the country.
  • Using a hint reduces the points earned for a correct answer to half a point (0.5).

Game End:

  • The game concludes when all country shape images have been displayed.
  • A final message will display your total score and the time taken to complete the game.

Enjoy testing your geography skills and exploring the world through country shapes! How many countries can you correctly identify by their outline?
